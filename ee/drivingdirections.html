How to get to FDC @ UST ENGR

GPS Instructions:

  • For coordinates, use this: 44.940, -93.194
  • Use this address for GPS directions: 40 S. Cretin Ave, St Paul MN, 55105. You will have to enter it manually, and note that it is S cretin ave, not Cretin Ave S (yes, it does matter, for some reason)
  • Do NOT believe the search results for St Thomas and use the recommended 2115 Summit address, it will take you to the wrong place.


From Cretin & Grand intersection:   where Grand Ave makes a T into Cretin Ave (and becomes the south campus driveway)

  • Go west into the south campus driveway.
  • You will see a parking ramp on your left, and a greenhouse on your right.
  • FDC is the first building on the left after the parking ramp.
  • My office is on the 2nd floor of FDC, room 215. There are stairwell entrances at the north and west sides of the building. The North entrance has an elevator.

From I-94 or other approach from the north:   Map to UST from I-94

  • Use the Cretin/Vandalia Exit (just east of Hwy 280, midway between Minneapolis & St Paul downtowns).

  • Head south on Cretin.
    Pass the golf course and stoplight at Marshall.
    After a few blocks you'll pass the UST football stadium on your left.
    Go through stoplights at Summit Ave (a parkway with grass median).
    I am located in Facilities Design Center (FDC), just west of the intersection of Cretin Ave and Grand Ave.

  • One block after Summit is a stoplight at Grand. Turn right (west) into the parking lot.
    You will see a parking ramp on your left, and a greenhouse on your right.

  • FDC is the first building on the left after the parking ramp.
    My office is on the 2nd floor of FDC, room 215. There are stairwell entrances at the north and west sides of the building. The North entrance has an elevator.


From MSP Airport or other south/west origin:   Map to UST from MSP Airport

  • Take Hwy 5 heading north past the airport (the river will be on your right, airport on your left).
    Just after the airport exits, follow signs for St Paul, Hwy 5 (left lanes)
    Pass the Hwy 55 exits, follow under Fort Snelling and cross the mississippi bridge (do NOT take the mendota bridge exit)

  • Immediately after the bridge, exit on Edgecumbe (exit is also labeled for Shepard Rd).
    The ramp splits, keep left toward Edgecumbe (not Shepard). This heads you over Hwy 5 and northbound on Edgecumbe.
    Follow Edgecumbe for about a half mile.

  • Just after the railroad crossing, at the light, turn left on St Paul Ave.
    St paul Ave curves right and merges to become Cleveland Ave.
    Follow Cleveland north, through highland village commercial area, past St Kates.

  • Turn left At Grand Ave (stoplight, SA station, Davanni's Pizza)
    Follow west on Grand two blocks to the light at Cretin and continue straight into the parking lot.
    You will see a parking ramp on your left, and a greenhouse on your right.

  • FDC is the first building on the left after the parking ramp.
    My office is on the 2nd floor of FDC, room 215. There are stairwell entrances at the north and west sides of the building. The North entrance has an elevator.
    My office is across from the vending machines in LL20.
